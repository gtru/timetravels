About

Doc Brown’s Time Travels was founded by doctor Emmett Brown, important scientist and inventor who has distinguish himself by the deep, pioneering and innovative interest in time travel: his invention is the Time Machine, a car that, thanks to its “flux capacitor”new technology, allows its driver and passengers to travel across space-time continuum, backwards or forwards to any point in history.

At Doc Brown's Time Travels we are committed to offer a quality experience from start to finish. Our travels are meticulously planned by art, history, paleontology and archaeology experts. It is possible to choose a guide lecturer or travel alone to conduct studies independently.

These are amongst the most respected and renowned experts and academics today. This is our highly qualified team, competent and efficient.
