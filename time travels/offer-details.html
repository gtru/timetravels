The origin of the universe

Until a few decades ago, the most popular theory of our universe's origin was centered on a cosmic cataclysm unmatched in all of history—the big bang. This theory was born of the observation that other galaxies are moving away from our own at great speed, in all directions, as if they had all been propelled by an ancient explosive force.

Obviously that theory, frivolous and original as it was, it's now proved wrong since we built our first time-travel machine and we actually went back there.

The words "Big Bang Theory" can only be found in history books or in funny TV shows.

Come aboard and travel back in time to this majestic event!

Remember: Some of the billions and billions of atoms colliding and bouncing everywhere in the cosmo are now part of you. Terrifying isn't it?
